About the Authors

Re-Connect is the Result of Over 15 Years of Study


Re-Connect was born from the need to solve seemingly unsolvable pains, based on years of exploration across the corners of the world.

OUR HISTORY

The Pursuit of a Fit Body

We began with the desire to have a healthy and strong body, obtaining certifications such as PT, Let's Bands Level 1 & 2, TRX, and Animal Flow.

Rehabilitation of Biomechanical Issues

We dedicated ourselves to biomechanical rehabilitation through modules such as Dimifitness Movement, Trigger Point, fascial lines, TRX, and performance training.

Understanding the Mind

We deepened our knowledge in anthropology, psychoanalysis, NLP and Theta Healing, yet encountered a gap that compelled us to turn to Ancient Teachings and Religions.

Healing the Main Systems

We explored certifications and methods such as Nutrition, Oxygen Advantage and plant-based medicine to address issues in the digestive, respiratory, and emotional systems, realizing that true control over our health is not in the hands of others.

Healing the Soul & Unveiling the Absolute Truths

We sought answers in the mountains, from elders and children – the true masters of life – in Asia, in Buddhism, Zen, Hinduism, and Christianity. Beyond separation, seeing them as treasures of wisdom, in all we discover the Secrets of Life.
